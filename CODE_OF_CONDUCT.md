# Code of Conduct / 行为准则

[English](#english) · [中文](#中文)

## English

We want Skia Painter to be a welcoming, harassment-free space for everyone, regardless of experience, background or identity.

**Expected behavior:** be respectful and constructive, assume good intent, accept feedback gracefully, and focus on what is best for the project and community.

**Unacceptable behavior:** harassment, insults, discriminatory or sexualized language, personal or political attacks, publishing others' private information, or any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate.

**Scope:** this applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions — and when representing the project in public.

**Reporting:** report concerns by opening a confidential issue or contacting the maintainer. Maintainers may remove, edit or reject contributions and comments that violate this code, and may temporarily or permanently ban any contributor for behavior they deem inappropriate.

This document is adapted in spirit from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).

## 中文

我们希望 Skia Painter 成为一个对所有人友好、无骚扰的空间，无论经验、背景或身份如何。

**期望的行为：** 尊重他人、保持建设性、善意揣测、平和接受反馈，并以项目和社区的最大利益为重。

**不可接受的行为：** 骚扰、侮辱、歧视性或性化的言辞、人身或政治攻击、公开他人隐私信息，或任何合理判断为不当的行为。

**适用范围：** 适用于所有项目空间（issue、PR、讨论），以及在公开场合代表本项目时。

**举报：** 如有问题，可提交保密 issue 或联系维护者。维护者有权移除、编辑或拒绝违反本准则的贡献与评论，并可对其认为不当的行为者临时或永久封禁。

本文档在精神上参考了 [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/)。
